Banana Bread

Simple yet tasty, making banana bread for a snack or dessert is something everyone should knoe how to make.

Follow this simple instructions to learn how to make the best banana bread you and your friends are gona taste.

These are the ingredients you need

  • 3 bananas, the browner the better.
  • 1 and a half cup all-purpose flour.
  • 1 cup of procesed oatmeal.
  • Between 1/2 and 1 cup of white sugar, to taste.
  • 1/4 cup of vegetal oil
  • chocolate chips, nuts or rasings, 3.5 oz

Mash the bananas, add all the other ingredients in whatever order, pre-heat the oven in low and let ir cook for 45 to 60 minutes!

Let it cool before eating it!
